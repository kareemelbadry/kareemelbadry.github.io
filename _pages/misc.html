On publishing

I don't think scientists should have to pay to publish their work and am a big supporter of The Open Journal of Astrophysics, where I have recently focused much of my refereeing and publishing. Feel free to contact me if you are curious about this publishing model.

On running

I like to run. I very occasionally run races, although I much prefer to run for free. If you are in town or we are at a conference together, I'd love to join you for a run!
